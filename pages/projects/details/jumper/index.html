Jumper

Jumper is a game I write in my spare time. It's made for the 3ds and coded in C

At the moment it's mearly a prototype, there isn't much there jet, but I work on changing that.

Right now you can allready scroll the background with the shoulder buttons and rotate a sprite in the middle of the screen with the pad.

Also there are up to 1000 free moving sprites on the screen. This is just for perfomance testing and will be removed later, but from that you can see that a large amount of objects won't make any problems.

The textures are not from me, but I will add my own textures after the subsystem is finished. Notch and Andrew (President of RE-LOGIC), please don't sue me
