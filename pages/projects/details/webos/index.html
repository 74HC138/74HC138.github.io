WebOS

"WebOs,huh...", you say

"What can it do?", you say

Nothing, but a lot, I say

The OS itself can't do that much, heck it doesn't even has desktop. You know what it got that I havn't seen in any other OS? Web! Actually thats not true. I've seen other OSes that can 'browse' the web, but heck, not like this. In this OS you don't have local programs, you got servers with programs and when you need a programm you ping a server. You can have programs, that load from a server and load more things from different servers. You don't have space on your Advanced Computer, heck, try cloud

Installation is easy with the installer. Just one command and finished. Upgrading is easy to. Just drop the update files on a server and ping it with every computer, done!
